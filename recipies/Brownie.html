Brownie

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of powdered sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Butter
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • Salt as per req
  • A LOT OF ICE CREAM preferably vanilla :p

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a separate bowl and liquidy ingredients on another bowl
  2. Then mix both the ingredients together and mix until the batter becomes thick enough
  3. Spread a parchment paper in a 8x8 inch baking pan and pour the batter on the baking pan
  4. Preheat the oven for a bit and then transfer the baking pan, bake it at 325 Fahrenheit for 40 to 45 mins
  5. To know whether the dish is done, check it using a toothpick and if it comes out with few crumbs attached it's done
  6. Now let it rest until it's not TOO hot, then slice it up scoop up some ice cream and enjoy :D
  7. You can store the rest and just heat them for a while in the oven and enjoy it hehe
